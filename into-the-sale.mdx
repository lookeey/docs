## See new tokens

You may see new tokens appear in the main page as they are *bumped*. Bumping a token makes it go to the top of the list, increasing its visibility.

## Buy and sell tokens

By clicking a token's card in the main page, you will be redirected to its page.
From there, you are able to see its chart, buy, sell, bump and comment in an intuitive interface.
